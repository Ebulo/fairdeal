01 Revenue Department

Odisha Land Mutation Processes & Revenue Workflows

Key Process Steps:
  • Application at Tahasil office through Form I and II.
  • Revenue Inspector site verification in 7-15 working days.
  • Sub-Registrar mutation order issuance.
  • RoR update in the Bhulekh portal.
Failure Points We De-Risk:
  • Duplicate plot numbers across Khata records.
  • Boundary disputes during RI inspection.
  • Missing legal heir declarations on inherited land.
  • Incomplete stamp-duty payment records.

02 Title Integrity

Khata Separation & Legal Title-Chain Integrity
